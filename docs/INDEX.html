Quick Reference — Key Concepts
📐 INR Number Format
All revenue values use Indian format:
₹750 → ₹750
₹45,000 → ₹45K
₹4,50,000 → ₹4.5L
₹1,20,00,000 → ₹1.2Cr
🏥 Health Segments
🏆 Champion — score ≥ 0.80, drop < 10%
✅ Healthy — score ≥ 0.60
⚠️ At Risk — score ≥ 0.40
🔴 Critical — score < 0.40
🔄 Data Refresh
Partner features: 5 min TTL
Clustering: On demand
Churn / Credit: Full mode only
Association rules: 30 min TTL
⚡ Revenue at Risk
Probability-weighted loss formula:
At Risk = churn_prob × 90d_revenue
Applied as fallback when full scoring is unavailable (FAST_MODE).
